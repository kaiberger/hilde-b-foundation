Our Mission

The Hilde B Foundation is committed to eliminating domestic violence from all American households by 2035. We provide resources for victims, support domestic violence shelters, and advocate for a future free of abuse.

Get Help Now Support Our Mission

About Us

Headquartered in Santa Clara, California, the Hilde B Foundation is dedicated to ending domestic violence in the U.S. by 2035. We work towards immediate aid for victims, support for shelters, and raising awareness to break the cycle of abuse.

Get Help

If you or someone you know is experiencing domestic violence, help is available.

  • National Domestic Violence Hotline: 800-799-7233
  • Text: “START” to 88788
  • Online Chat: thehotline.org

Donate

Join us in creating a world free from domestic violence. Your donation will provide emergency shelter, counseling, and legal aid.
